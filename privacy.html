WELCOME TO IJEMSS

PRIVACY POLICIES

IJEMSS is dedicated to safeguarding the privacy of its visitors, users, and authors. We have established the following privacy guidelines to protect user information. Please note that IJEMSS reserves the right to modify these guidelines as necessary. Any updates will be posted on our website, and users may not receive direct notifications of policy changes. The personal information collected by IJEMSS depends on how you interact with our site. We may gather certain non-personally identifiable details, such as your operating system, browser type, and the domain name of your Internet Service Provider. If personally identifiable information is required, we may collect it through contact details you provide to us. IJEMSS does not share, rent, or sell your personal information to third parties, except to deliver requested products or services with your consent. We use personal information in ways aligned with its intended purposes, including enabling site use, responding to inquiries, system administration, customer support, site and journal improvement, and enforcing our Terms and Conditions. In certain situations, IJEMSS may disclose information to prevent or investigate suspected fraud, illegal activities, potential threats to physical safety, or as required by law. Our site may contain links to external websites and organizations, which are not under our control. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies and terms of these external sites. This privacy policy pertains solely to IJEMSS.

SECURITY

IJEMSS is dedicated to safeguarding your personal information. To prevent unauthorized access and disclosure, we implement comprehensive electronic, physical, and managerial measures to secure the personal data we collect. Access to personal data is strictly controlled, ensuring that only authorized personnel can view or use it. All personal data is maintained in a highly secure computing environment, protected by advanced security systems and firewalls to prevent unauthorized access. We employ Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology with 256-bit RSA encryption. This ensures that all information transmitted between your computer and our secure servers is encrypted, making it unreadable to anyone during transit.
